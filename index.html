Lifestyle

The ocean is an important part to me and I like to spend my spare time surfing, kiteboarding, lifeguarding and racing Inflatable Rescue Boats (IRB's). The work/life balance is very important!

The passion for technology came back in 2013 (age 16) when East End's Social Media presence was non-existent and communication to members was through email and word of mouth. With time being limited for volunteers, I sought to improve this. I created East End's Facebook page to advertise the club to the public and used Facebook Groups to engage members effectively. This helped contribute to the success of increasing membership over the years. In 2017, 2018 and 2019 East End were the Top Club in Taranaki. The page has over 500-1000 engagements with the public on its daily posts and is now run by 4 other administrators.

During my Club Captain and Lifeguard Co-Ordinator Volunteer roles at the club, there were no fatalities, fortunately.
